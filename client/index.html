Other Information

Pirate Party Australia may in the future create state branches, and to ease in this process, we would like your consent to include your Pirate Party Australia membership for the registration of any of these branches.

This allows us to ensure that you are represented on the Federal level, the State level and the Local level in elections.

Pirate Party Australia does not allow membership of multiple Australian federal political parties. If you wish to sign up to Pirate Party Australia and are currently a member of another party, please resign from that party first before submitting your application.
